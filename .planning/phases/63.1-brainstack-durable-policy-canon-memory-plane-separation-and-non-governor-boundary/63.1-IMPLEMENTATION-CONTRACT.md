# Phase 63.1 Implementation Contract

## execution intent

Create a Brainstack-owned canonical durable policy/preference authority surface and explicit memory-plane boundaries, while freezing that Brainstack may inform governance but may not itself become governor.

## required architecture discipline

### 1. canonical policy must be explicit

- durable policy authority must use explicit Brainstack-owned records or snapshots
- it must not rely on transcript residue being "close enough"

### 2. memory planes must stay distinct

- the practical authority model is:
  - `canonical policy` for explicit long-lived user/project rules
  - `operating/live state` for current commitments and current factual runtime truth
  - `evidence` for transcript/continuity support
- procedural skill memory is reusable method/workflow support, not canonical policy
- native compatibility memory is bounded support context, not the main doctrine store

### 3. promotion must be narrow and explicit

- only explicit durable rules may become canonical policy authority
- default behavior is `no promotion unless explicit`
- do not auto-promote vague importance, assistant recap, speculative summaries, or assistant-inferred doctrine

### 4. no heuristic importance engine

- this phase must not solve "what is important?" by keyword lists, language-specific cues, or hidden heuristic scoring
- acceptable promotion is based on explicit typed source shape and explicit rule class, not vibes

### 4.1 minimal-topology rule

- do not introduce a new storage topology unless it materially clarifies canonical ownership
- prefer the smallest Brainstack-owned extension that gives one explicit canonical durable policy authority surface

### 5. Brainstack is not governor

- Brainstack may project policy truth
- it may not execute, schedule, approve, deny, or otherwise act as the runtime governor
- later runtime phases may consume the policy snapshot, but the enforcement boundary remains outside Brainstack

### 6. runtime handoff must be read-only here

- this phase may define the read shape the runtime will later consume
- it must not implement runtime intake/execution/governance inside Brainstack

### 7. no shadow duplication

- the final design must avoid leaving the same doctrine split ambiguously across multiple equal-authority planes
- supporting copies may remain, but the canonical owner must be explicit

### 8. multimodal discipline

- the policy lane and projection shape must not assume text-only future inputs
- canonical policy should remain expressible as typed records, metadata, and normalized rule payloads

### 9. closeout truth rule

- closeout must say exactly:
  - where canonical durable policy now lives
  - what other planes still contain only supporting evidence
  - what later runtime phases may consume
  - what Brainstack still does not own

## required design decisions to freeze before execution

- exact canonical durable policy record family or owner
- exact projection/snapshot surface
- exact rule-promotion criteria
- exact evidence-only criteria
- exact practical authority-model table
- exact non-governor statement for later phases to inherit

## accepted change shapes

- Brainstack record-family additions
- policy projection additions
- memory-plane role clarifications
- retrieval/projection ordering changes that strengthen canonical policy authority
- proof-oriented tests around policy authority and plane separation

## rejected change shapes

- runtime intake/execution logic
- transcript scraping promoted as policy architecture
- heuristic "importance" classifiers
- replacing all skill usage with policy records
- making Brainstack decide approval or execution

## proof obligations for the later execute step

- show an explicit durable rule landing in canonical Brainstack policy state
- show that transcript/continuity may still preserve evidence without being sole authority
- show that the memory-plane role table is actually enforceable in projection/retrieval
- show that the read-only runtime snapshot exists without turning Brainstack into governor

## inspector note

This phase is the point where Brainstack stops being "a place where the rule happened to appear" and becomes "the explicit canonical owner of durable policy truth" without crossing into runtime governance.
