- the task/commitment owner must be explicit and singular
- assistant language must match the real owner contract
- due-date semantics must be canonicalized against the user timezone
- retrieval must be structured and truth-based before transcript fallback
- optionality and status must survive reset and recall

## Minimum acceptable outcomes

- one explicit task/commitment architecture is chosen and implemented

- reset-era lookup of date-scoped tasks works through the chosen owner

## Prohibited shortcuts

- transcript/continuity FTS as the main task store
- fake task persistence through assistant wording alone
- ambiguous dual ownership between Brainstack and an external owner
